THINGS WE ARE NOT PERFECT AT
We are always looking for more sustainable solutions within our business, including finding new ways to incorporate recycled materials into our products. Below are some aspects of our business that could be improved and made more sustainable.
TRAVELLING, ESPECIALLY LONG FLIGHTS.
We are fully aware of the impact that air travel has on the environment. However, in many cases there is no other alternative (i.e. train). Photography in different countries is an important part of our brand as it allows us to connect with a global audience.
SHIPPING.
There are many flaws in the shipping industry and we ship to customers all over the world. We use emissions-free shipping for deliveries in the UK, but there is still room for improvement.
RETURNS.
We don't have many returns, but we try to minimize returns as much as possible. Most of our returns are due to incomplete or incorrect addresses, so we are working on a better system to avoid this.
OUR SHIPPING & RETURNS METHODS
Our packaging is plastic-free and our garment bags, mailers and shipping labels are made from 100% biodegradable materials that break down in 18 months and 3-6 months when composted. Our sunglasses are shipped in recycled cardboard. We also use a third-party service that collects and recycles all cardboard boxes and packaging we receive from inventory deliveries.

When a customer places multiple orders, he always ships all items in one package to minimize the need for extra shipping. We also reduce shipping by having in-house sample and pattern developers who create final products without the need for shipping or flying prior to mass production.
RESTORE THE EARTH
In 2023, we launched RESTORE THE EARTH. Instead of donating funds to other foundations, we decided to create our own foundation to ensure that the funds and resources go to the right places and are used in the most impactful way possible.

Setting up your own foundation is a long and tedious process that requires passing multiple checks and criteria beforehand. We are currently in the final stages of the process and awaiting approval.

Once our foundation is approved and officially established, we will announce our first mission, which will begin in early 2023.
